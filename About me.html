ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Krish—a passionate web developer who blends creativity with code to craft beautiful, responsive, and meaningful digital experiences. My journey began with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’ve since mastered Flexbox, Grid, media queries, and animation techniques to bring websites to life. Whether it’s building interactive layouts, troubleshooting performance issues, or optimizing user experience, I approach every challenge with curiosity and persistence.

I love working on projects that celebrate family milestones and personal stories, infusing each site with emotion and purpose. I’m actively exploring frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind, and diving deep into Python to build custom tools—including a Jarvis-style assistant with voice interaction and system control.

My goal is to create websites that aren’t just functional, but unforgettable. I care deeply about accessibility, responsiveness, and user delight. From confetti animations to immersive galleries, I’m always experimenting with new ways to elevate the web.
